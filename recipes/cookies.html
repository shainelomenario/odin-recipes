Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies

A plate of freshly baked cookies, golden-brown with a soft, chewy texture, arranged neatly on a white ceramic plate. Some cookies are studded with melted chocolate chips, adding a touch of indulgence. The scene is cozy and inviting, perfect for a sweet treat.

Cookie Dough Ingredients

  • Chocolate Chips
  • Brown & White Sugars
  • Cornstarch
  • Salt
  • Baking Soda
  • Melted Butter
  • Vanilla
  • Egg Yolk
  • Egg
  • Flour

Steps

To make chewy chocolate chip cookies, follow these concise steps:

  1. Melt Butter: Melt ¾ cup (170g) unsalted butter and let it cool for 5 minutes.
  2. Mix Sugars and Butter: In a medium bowl, whisk together the melted butter, ¾ cup (150g) packed light or dark brown sugar, and ½ cup (100g) granulated sugar until smooth.
  3. Add Egg and Vanilla: Whisk in 1 large egg, 1 egg yolk, and 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract until combined.
  4. Combine Dry Ingredients: In a separate large bowl, whisk together 2¼ cups (280g) all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon baking soda, 1½ teaspoons cornstarch, and ½ teaspoon salt.
  5. Combine Wet and Dry Mixtures: Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix with a large spoon or rubber spatula until a soft, thick, and slightly greasy dough forms.
  6. Fold in Chocolate Chips: Gently fold in 1¼ cups (225g) semi-sweet chocolate chips.
  7. Chill Dough: Cover the dough tightly and refrigerate for at least 2–3 hours, or up to 3–4 days.
  8. Preheat Oven: When ready to bake, preheat the oven to 325°F (163°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper or silicone baking mats.
  9. Shape Cookies: Let the dough sit at room temperature for 10 minutes. Scoop about 3 tablespoons of dough per cookie, roll into balls, and place them on the prepared baking sheets, spacing them about 3 inches apart.
  10. Bake: Bake for 12–13 minutes, or until the edges are lightly browned. The centers will look soft.
  11. Cool: Remove from the oven and let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

After that's done, pour a glass and milk and enjoy your chewy chocolate chip cookies!
